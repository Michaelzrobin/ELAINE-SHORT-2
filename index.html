Elaine Short

Hi! I’m Elaine. I’m a writer, editor and comic maker in Chicago, IL.

You might know me from a little book I wrote and illustrated called Let’s Be Awkward Together, based off my webcomic Funny Girl. Or perhaps you saw my cartoon for Cards Against Humanity’s 12 Days of Holiday Bullshit Funny Pages Zine. Maybe you’ve participated in 24 Hour Bookclub, a Twitter-based book club I help organize. Oh wait, I think we follow each other on Twitter, right? Yeah, you were the one who couldn’t stop tweeting about Miley.

I’m an associate editor at a publishing firm in Chicago’s River North where I mostly write about boats. Before that, I wrote for Bright Bright Great, a kick-ass Chicago design firm, Maxistentialism, Inc.–where I wrote a queefing joke or two and helped write the new version of Werewolf–and several technical and trade publications. But you know that, right, because we’re like, pals, right?

Wait, we’re not? You weren’t the one tweeting incessantly about Miley? That’s a relief, because I totally unfollowed that person. In that case, shoot me an email at short.elaine@gmail.com.

I like writing about all kinds of things, and if you are in need of a freelance writer or editor, I’d love to hear from you. Or if you need a friend. I’m down for that too.

Have a good one.
